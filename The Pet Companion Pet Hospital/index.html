Welcome to The Pet Companion Pet Hospital

Your pet's health is our top priority.

We provide the best care for your furry friends.

Excellence in care is our priority.

The Pet Companion Pet Hospital is proud to be American Animal Hospital Association (AAHA) accredited since 1979. Only about 12% of animal hospitals are accredited in the United States and Canada. We dedicate all of our time & energy to helping & caring for your companions. We see dogs, cats, birds, small mammals, reptiles, and exotics.
