About Us

The Pet Companion Pet Hospital is proud to be recognized as one of the leading veterinary hospitals in the region.

The Pet Companion Pet Hospital offers a variety of services.

We provide comprehensive veterinary services, including 24/7 emergency care, routine wellness check-ups, and advanced oncology treatments to ensure your pet's health and happiness.

Emergency Care


We provide 24/7 emergency care, including advanced surgical procedures, to ensure your pet receives immediate and comprehensive treatment when it matters most.

Regular Visits & Check-ups


We also provide regular visits to monitor your pet's health, administer vaccinations, and offer preventative care to ensure a long and happy life for your furry friends.

Oncology & Internal Medicine


Our oncology team offers advanced cancer treatments, including chemotherapy and radiation therapy. We are also equipped to provide accurate diagnoses and personalized care plans to support your pet's health and well-being.

Our facility is equipped with state-of-the art technology and equipment.

Our facility is equipped with cutting-edge technology and advanced equipment, enabling us to deliver precise diagnoses and effective treatments tailored to your pet's needs.

Regenerative Medicine


We offer regenerative medicine treatments, including stem cell therapy and platelet-rich plasma (PRP) therapy, to promote healing and improve your pet's quality of life.

Laser Therapy


Our laser therapy services provide non-invasive pain relief and promote healing for various conditions, including arthritis, post-surgical recovery, and soft tissue injuries.

Digital Radiography


Our digital radiography system allows for quick and accurate imaging, enabling us to diagnose and treat your pet's conditions with precision and efficiency.

Accredited with Excellence Since 1979

We are proud to uphold the highest standards in veterinary care as an American Animal Hospital Association (AAHA) accredited hospital, ensuring your pets receive exceptional care.
