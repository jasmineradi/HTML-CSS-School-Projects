Open Saturdays 8am - 1pm

Join us for a delightful experience at the Cass County Farmers Market! We offer a wide variety of fresh produce, homemade goods, and local crafts. Our market is open every Saturday from 8am to 1pm, rain or shine.

We are located at the Cass County Fairgrounds. Come support local farmers and artisans while enjoying a fun day out with family and friends!
