Welcome to the Orchard

About Us

We are a family-owned orchard located in the heart of the countryside. Our apples are grown with love and care, ensuring the best quality for our customers.

Our Apples

We offer a variety of apples, including Fuji, Gala, and Granny Smith. Each apple is handpicked at the peak of ripeness to ensure maximum flavor.

Our apples are perfect for eating fresh, baking, or making cider. Visit us to pick your own apples or buy them pre-picked from our farm stand.

Tractor Tours

Join us for a tractor tour of the orchard! Our knowledgeable guides will take you through the fields, sharing information about our apple varieties and farming practices.

Tractor tours are available on weekends and holidays. Book your spot today!
